ISP Description

A clip-on roller skates that is electric. Using an accelerometer, we will enable the future of foot transportation. The bulk of the project will be split between the mechanical aspects, refining and removing excess parts, and the software/electrical parts. The goal of the project is to have a completely self designed electric rollerskates with top speed of 1 mile per hour.
