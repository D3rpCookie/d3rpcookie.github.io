Computer Science I

A course which provides the backbone of the field of computer science. Mainly discussing both the physics computer architecture and also analysis, design and implementation of programs in Swift.

Computer Science II

Build on the previous course of CS I to dive into the topics in a deeper level, covering additional data structures, sorting algorithms, and object-orientated programming.

Computer Science III

Uses the previous knowledge gain from CS I and CS II to explore large projects in a group setting, and gain deeper programing experiences in variety of languages, but mainly focusing on Swift.
